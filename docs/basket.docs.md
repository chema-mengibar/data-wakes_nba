# Basket


## Positions

### Center
- The tallest player on the team
- short jumpers, hook shots, and dunks

###  Point Guard
- hortest player
- best passer and ball handler
- most assists and steals

### Shooting Guard
- best shooter
  

### Small Forward
- all-purpose player on offense

### Power Forward
- re-bounder
- much like a center
